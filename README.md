# 📊 Stock Market Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview

This project presents an **interactive stock market analysis dashboard** that provides insights into stock performance, sector trends, and top-performing/underperforming companies.

The dashboard is designed to help users quickly understand:

* Market trends
* Sector-wise performance
* Top gainers and losers
* Detailed stock-level exploration

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

* **Average Stock Price:** 2.85K
* **Overall Market Change:** +37.94%

These indicators suggest a **positive but uneven market movement**, driven by selective sectors rather than broad-based growth.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ Market Overview

* Displays aggregated stock data
* Includes:

  * Company name
  * Sector
  * Latest traded price (LTP)
  * Percentage change

🔍 **Insight:**

* Market growth is **highly concentrated**
* Few sectors contribute heavily to overall gains

---

### 2️⃣ Top Gainers & Losers

#### 🟢 Top Gainers

* Adani Power Limited (+407%)
* JSW Energy Limited (+403%)
* REC Limited (+300%)
* Bajaj Auto Limited (+219%)

💡 **Observation:**

* Power and energy stocks dominate gains
* Indicates **sector-specific rally**

#### 🔴 Top Losers

* United Spirits Limited (−284%)
* CG Power (−161%)
* Avenue Supermarts (−159%)
* Bajaj Finance (−128%)

⚠️ **Observation:**

* Consumer and FMCG sectors are underperforming
* Indicates **weak demand or profit booking**

---

### 3️⃣ Sector Insights

| Sector                   | Performance |

---

### 4️⃣ Stock Explorer

Provides a **detailed breakdown of individual stocks**, including:

* Symbol
* Company name
* Sector
* LTP
* % Change
* Volume
* Time filters (Year, Quarter, Month, Day)

🎯 Enables:

* Deep stock-level analysis
* Filtering by sector or company

---

## 📈 Overall Insights

* Market is **bullish but not balanced**
* Gains are **momentum-driven**, not broad-based
* Heavy reliance on:

  * Power
  * Energy
  * Auto sectors
* Weakness in:

  * FMCG
  * Consumer goods
  * Breweries

## ⚠️ Limitations

* Data appears aggregated (Sum of LTP, % Change)
* Extreme % values may indicate:

  * Data scaling issues
  * Aggregation distortion
