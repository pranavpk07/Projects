
# 📈 Project: NSE Live Stock Prices Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes **live stock price movements from the National Stock Exchange (NSE)** to identify sector-wise trends, market concentration, and momentum-driven behavior.

The focus is not just tracking prices — but understanding **where the market is actually moving and why**.

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

* 📊 Real-time stock price tracking
* 📈 Sector-wise performance analysis
* 🔥 Identification of top gainers and losers
* 📉 Market concentration insights
* 📊 Trend-based decision support

---

## 📊 Insights

### 1. Market Concentration

* Market gains are **heavily concentrated**
* Driven mainly by:

  * Power
  * Energy
  * 2/3 Wheelers (**+469% combined growth**)

👉 Reality: This is not a healthy rally — it's **narrow and fragile**.

---

### 2. Momentum-Driven Rally

* Top gainers contribute **+2608% total returns**

👉 Translation: A few stocks are doing all the heavy lifting.
This is classic **momentum chasing**, not stable growth.

---

### 3. Weak Sectors

* Underperforming sectors:

  * Consumer
  * FMCG
  * Breweries & Distilleries (**−284%**)

* Major losers:

  * United Spirits (**−161%**)
  * CG Power (**−128%**)

👉 Insight: Money is rotating out of defensive/consumer sectors.

---

### 4. Uneven Sector Participation

* Top 3 sectors contribute **+1060%+ combined**
* Total sector movement: **3794%**

👉 Problem: Market participation is **imbalanced**, increasing risk of correction.

---

## ⚠️ Core Observations

* Market strength is **not broad-based**
* High dependency on a few outperforming sectors
* Weak sectors continue to decline without recovery signals
* Traditional “safe” sectors are underperforming
